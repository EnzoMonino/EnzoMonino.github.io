Welcome!

My name is Enzo Monino, I am currently a post-doc in the group of [Giuseppe Carleo](https://www.epfl.ch/labs/cqsl/) at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) in Switzerland. My previous post-doc was in the group of [Libor Veis](http://libor.veisovi.com/) at the [J. Heyrovsky Institute of Physical Chemistry](https://jh-inst.cas.cz/) in Prague (Czech Republic). I did my Ph.D. in the group of [Pierre-François Loos](https://pfloos.github.io/WEB_LOOS/) at the [Laboratoire de Chimie et Physique Quantiques](https://www.lcpq.ups-tlse.fr/?lang=en) in Toulouse (France).

My principal focus is the development of new electronic structure methods for the description of molecular systems. I have been working with various frameworks, Green's function methods for the study of charged and neutral excited states in molecules, the Density Matrix Renormalization Group (DMRG) method and its application on strongly correlated systems. Recently, I have been interested in embedding methods with the perspective of studying large strongly correlated systems. In this line of research, I have worked in the application and improvement of DMRG in a DFT embedding framework. 

I am currently working on the application of Neural Quantum States (NQS) (a variational approach based on neural networks) for fermionic systems and its incorporation in embedding methods.

I also have a strong interest in tensor networks and their usage in quantum chemistry as well as the study and application of different mathematical concepts in quantum chemistry.
